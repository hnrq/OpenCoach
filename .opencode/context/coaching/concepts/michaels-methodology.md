# Concept: Michaels Methodology

**Purpose**: High-protein, high-intensity metabolic coaching.

---

## Core Idea
The Michaels methodology prioritizes muscle retention through high protein (2.2g/kg LBM) and systemic metabolic stress via "Metabolic Primers" before strength blocks.

## Key Points
- **Metabolic Primers**: 5-10 min high-intensity circuits (AMRAP/Tabata) to elevate heart rate.
- **Protein Floor**: Never drop below 2.2g of protein per kg of Lean Body Mass.
- **WROC Tracking**: Adjust calories only after analyzing 14 days of Weekly Rate of Change.
- **RPE 8-9**: Strength blocks must stay in the "challenging but sustainable" intensity zone.

## Quick Example (Macros for 80kg User at 15% BF)
- LBM: 68kg
- Protein: 150g (68 * 2.2)
- Fats: 64g (80 * 0.8)
- Carbs: Variable based on training intensity.
